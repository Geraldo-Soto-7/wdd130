Rafting Site Plan

Gerardo Soto

The Whitewater Rafting website is a comprehensive resource for rafting enthusiasts, providing detailed information to help users book their ideal rafting trips.

Purpose

The Whitewater Rafting website aims to educate and inspire rafting enthusiasts by providing comprehensive trip information, safety guidelines, and booking resources, fostering a connected and well-prepared rafting community.

Auidience

The target audience for The Whitewater Rafting website includes adventure seekers and outdoor enthusiasts, from beginners to experienced rafters, looking for detailed trip information, safety guidelines, and booking resources.It also caters to families and groups seeking memorable rafting adventures and individuals interested in learning more about the sport.
